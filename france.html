Basic Info

France, the epitome of romance and refinement, is a European gem known for its rich culture, art, cuisine, and fashion. From the elegant boulevards of Paris to the lavender fields of Provence, France offers a captivating mix of history and beauty. Explore medieval castles, alpine villages, sun-kissed beaches of the Riviera, and world-renowned vineyards. Whether you're interested in art at the Louvre, skiing in the Alps, or savoring fine wine and cheese, France promises an unforgettable experience.

Top Cities

  • Paris – Capital city with iconic landmarks and romantic charm
  • Lyon – Gourmet capital with historic architecture
  • Nice – Stunning coastal beauty on the French Riviera
  • Marseille – Port city with Mediterranean flair

Best Restaurants

Indulge in culinary masterpieces at Le Meurice in Paris (~€250–350 tasting menu), or enjoy rustic French cuisine at Bocuse in Lyon (~€100–150). Budget-friendly options include Chez Gladines (Paris – €15–30) for Basque dishes. Don’t miss the fresh croissants at Du Pain et des Idées for under €5.

Hotels

Luxury stays like Ritz Paris or Hotel Le Bristol offer iconic experiences (~€900–1,200/night). Hotel de France in Lyon or Hotel Negresco in Nice are lavish mid-tier options (~€200–400/night). Affordable chain hotels like ibis or B&B Hotels offer rooms from €60–100/night in most cities.

Flights

France's main international gateway is Charles de Gaulle Airport (CDG) in Paris. Flights from within Europe can be as low as €30–€100 with Ryanair or EasyJet. Intercontinental flights range from €500–1,000. Domestic routes between Paris, Marseille, Nice, and Lyon are well-served by Air France and budget carriers (~€60–150).

Tourist Spots

  • The Eiffel Tower – Paris’s iconic landmark
  • Louvre Museum – World’s most visited art museum
  • Palace of Versailles – Majestic royal residence
  • Mont Saint-Michel – Island commune and abbey
  • French Riviera – Beautiful beaches and luxury living
