Basic Info

Australia, the land down under, is known for its beautiful beaches, unique wildlife, and outdoor adventures. From the Great Barrier Reef to the Outback, Australia offers unforgettable experiences.

Top Cities

  • Sydney – Famous for the Opera House and beaches
  • Melbourne – Culture and coffee
  • Brisbane – Gateway to the Great Barrier Reef
  • Perth – Beautiful coastal city

Best Restaurants

From Sydney's seafood restaurants to Melbourne’s vibrant café culture, Australia is a food lover’s paradise.

Hotels

Stay at luxury resorts on the Gold Coast or boutique hotels in Melbourne's laneways.

Flights

Direct flights to Australia from major international airports in Asia, Europe, and the Americas.

Tourist Spots

  • Sydney Opera House
  • Great Barrier Reef
  • Uluru
  • Bondi Beach
  • Great Ocean Road
