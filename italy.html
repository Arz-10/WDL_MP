Basic Info

France is one of the world’s most visited countries, celebrated for its romantic atmosphere, exquisite cuisine, rich history, and iconic landmarks. From the bustling streets of Paris to the lavender fields of Provence and the glamorous beaches of the French Riviera, France offers something for every traveler. Known for its art, fashion, wines, and castles, it seamlessly blends modern elegance with old-world charm. Whether exploring medieval villages or sipping coffee under the Eiffel Tower, France enchants visitors with every step.

Top Cities

  • Paris – The capital city of lights and love, home to the Eiffel Tower
  • Lyon – Culinary capital with Roman ruins
  • Nice – Seaside city on the French Riviera
  • Marseille – Historic port with a vibrant cultural mix
  • Bordeaux – Famed for vineyards and charming architecture

Best Restaurants

Enjoy fine dining at the 3-Michelin-starred Le Meurice Alain Ducasse in Paris (~€200–400 per person). For mid-range delights, try Bistrot Paul Bert (~€30–60), known for its classic French steak-frites. Budget-friendly meals can be found at Le Bouillon Chartier (~€12–25), offering traditional fare in a historic setting. Don’t miss a crêpe from street vendors (~€5–8).

Hotels

Luxurious stays like Ritz Paris or Shangri-La Paris start at ~€800–1500 per night. Mid-range options such as Hotel Le Six or Hôtel de Londres range between €120–250/night. Budget hostels and boutique hotels can be found for €40–90 per night in cities like Lyon and Nice.

Flights

Main international airports include Charles de Gaulle (CDG) in Paris and Nice Côte d’Azur Airport. Flights from major European cities start at €50–100. From the US, fares range from €400–800 depending on the season. Domestic flights between cities are available (~€50–120), or you can travel via high-speed TGV trains.

Tourist Spots

  • Eiffel Tower – Symbol of France and Paris
  • Louvre Museum – World’s largest art museum
  • Mont Saint-Michel – Medieval island commune
  • Palace of Versailles – Lavish royal residence
  • French Riviera (Nice, Cannes, Monaco) – Coastal glamour and beaches
