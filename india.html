Basic Info

India is a land of diverse cultures, languages, and traditions. Known for its historical landmarks like the Taj Mahal, vibrant festivals, and unique landscapes, India offers a truly enriching travel experience.

Top Cities

  • Delhi – The bustling capital city
  • Jaipur – Known for its historic forts and palaces
  • Mumbai – India's financial capital
  • Goa – Famous for its beautiful beaches

Best Restaurants

India offers diverse cuisines from North to South, with street food, traditional meals, and world-class dining.

Hotels

From luxurious palaces in Rajasthan to peaceful resorts in Kerala, India offers various accommodation styles for travelers.

Flights

International flights to India can be taken to major cities like Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, and Chennai.

Tourist Spots

  • Taj Mahal
  • Jaipur Forts
  • Kerala Backwaters
  • Varanasi
  • Goa Beaches
