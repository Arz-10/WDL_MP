Basic Info

Russia, the largest country in the world by land area, spans Eastern Europe and Northern Asia. It's known for its diverse landscapes, rich history, and unique cultural heritage.

Top Cities

  • Moscow – The vibrant capital city
  • St. Petersburg – Known for its imperial architecture and canals
  • Novosibirsk – A major city in Siberia
  • Yekaterinburg – Located near the Ural Mountains
  • Kazan – Rich in Tatar culture

Best Restaurants

Explore traditional Russian cuisine, from hearty borscht and pelmeni to elegant dining experiences in Moscow and St. Petersburg.

Hotels

Find a range of accommodations, from luxurious hotels in major cities to cozy guesthouses in the countryside.

Flights

Major international airports in Russia include Domodedovo (Moscow), Sheremetyevo (Moscow), and Pulkovo (St. Petersburg).

Tourist Spots

  • Red Square and the Kremlin
  • Hermitage Museum
  • Lake Baikal
  • Peterhof Palace
  • St. Basil's Cathedral
