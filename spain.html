Basic Info

Spain is a country with a rich cultural history, famous for its art, architecture, and Mediterranean beaches. From the iconic Sagrada Familia to the lively streets of Madrid, Spain offers a vibrant experience for every traveler.

Top Cities

  • Madrid – The capital city, full of history and art
  • Barcelona – Known for its stunning architecture and beaches
  • Seville – Famous for flamenco and its beautiful old town
  • Valencia – A city of art and science, with beautiful beaches

Best Restaurants

Experience traditional Spanish cuisine, from tapas to paella, in the best restaurants across the country.

Hotels

Spain boasts a wide range of accommodations, from luxurious five-star hotels to cozy boutique stays.

Flights

Direct flights are available to Spain from major international airports, with Barcelona and Madrid being the main entry points.

Tourist Spots

  • Sagrada Familia
  • Park Guell
  • Alhambra
  • Madrid Royal Palace
  • Barcelona Beach
