Basic Info

Japan, an island country in East Asia, is famous for its blend of traditional culture and cutting-edge technology. From cherry blossoms to sushi, shrines to skyscrapers—Japan has it all!

Top Cities

  • Tokyo – The bustling capital city
  • Kyoto – Famous for temples and geishas
  • Osaka – Known for street food and nightlife
  • Hokkaido – Great for skiing and nature

Best Restaurants

Explore Michelin-star sushi spots in Tokyo, ramen alleys in Osaka, and authentic Kaiseki dining in Kyoto.

Hotels

Stay at luxury hotels in Tokyo, traditional ryokans in Kyoto, or capsule hotels for budget travelers.

Flights

Direct flights to Japan are available from major cities worldwide. Narita (Tokyo) and Kansai (Osaka) are the key international airports.

Tourist Spots

  • Mount Fuji
  • Arashiyama Bamboo Forest
  • Fushimi Inari Shrine
  • Tokyo Skytree
  • Nara Deer Park
