Basic Info

Egypt, the cradle of ancient civilization, is home to the world-renowned pyramids, the Sphinx, and the treasures of the pharaohs. Explore the wonders of the Nile and ancient tombs.

Top Cities

  • Cairo – Bustling capital with ancient treasures
  • Luxor – The world's greatest open-air museum
  • Alexandria – Mediterranean charm
  • Sharm El Sheikh – Famous for beaches and resorts

Best Restaurants

Explore traditional Egyptian cuisine like koshari and falafel in local eateries, or dine in luxury restaurants along the Nile.

Hotels

Luxury resorts by the Red Sea, historic hotels in Cairo, and boutique accommodations in Luxor.

Flights

Direct flights available to Cairo, Alexandria, and Sharm El Sheikh from major international cities.

Tourist Spots

  • Pyramids of Giza
  • Luxor Temple
  • Valley of the Kings
  • Abu Simbel
  • Egyptian Museum
